Know What music can do

Improves mood : Music can help regulate emotions, and create happiness and relaxation in everyday life.

Reduces stress : Listening to ‘relaxing’ music has been shown to reduce stress and anxiety

Improves exercise : Music can enhance aerobic exercise, boost mental and physical stimulation

Improves memory : Research has shown that the repetitive elements of rhythm and melody help our brains form patterns that enhance memory
